Grizzly Bear

Grizzly bears are the apex preditor in North America

  • Height: 9 feet tall
  • Size: Huge
  • Personality: Agressive
  • Color Brown

Grizzly bears live in the mountains and densly wooded areas in the west and north west of North America. They are territorial and will attack with out warning when threatened. Females with cubs are the most likely to attack an unaware hiker in the woods.
